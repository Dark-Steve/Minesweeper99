packages follow this organization:

data[0] width
data[1] height
data[2] bombCount

tile data offset by 5 bytes

byte[width * height]
top left -> bottom right

if revealed 0x10 (bit 4 0001 0000)
if revealed and bomb 0x10 and 0x20 (bit 4 and 5 0011 0000)
if revealed and not bomb 0x10 and numBombs (bit 4 and 0-3 0001 0???)
if not revealed and flag 0x40 (bit 6 0100 0000)
else 0 (0000 0000)
